## Decisions Made

- verify-phase is invoked MANUALLY by the human after execute-phase completes — it is NOT auto-triggered. This is the GSD pattern where execution and verification are separate steps at phase boundaries. The human has a chance to inspect documents before committing to verification.
- MANIFEST.md is generated by the verify-phase orchestrator directly (not via subagent) because the orchestrator already has the complete view of all documents verified and integrated. Spawning a subagent for this would add a Task() call for a task that requires no deep reasoning.
- DIGEST.md is written as the FINAL step (Step 8 after MANIFEST.md Step 7) to ensure the digest captures all verified documents, integration results, and the complete state. Writing it earlier (mid-analysis) would produce an incomplete digest (Pitfall 3 from research).
- Pipeline continues on gaps_found in both arch-verifier and arch-integrator: gaps are informational at phase-sign-off time. A MANIFEST.md showing which documents have gaps is more actionable than stopping before MANIFEST.md is produced. Only failed (can't run) and human_needed (human decision required) stop the pipeline.
